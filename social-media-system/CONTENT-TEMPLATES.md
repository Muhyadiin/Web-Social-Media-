# Content Templates & Hooks

Copy-paste templates to make posting fast. Fill the brackets, post, repurpose.

## Bio templates

**LinkedIn headline:**

> developers & contractors build smarter with accurate BOQs, takeoffs & cost estimates

**LinkedIn / IG bio:**
> 🏗️ Structural Engineering · Quantity Surveying · Cost Consulting
> 📐 Accurate BOQs, takeoffs & estimates — 13+ yrs
> 💬 Free quote within 1 business day
> 👇 Start here

## Hook formulas (first line = everything)

- **Mistake:** "5 mistakes that quietly blow your construction budget."
- **Number:** "I've costed 200+ projects. Here's the one habit that saves the most money."
- **Contrarian:** "Cheaper materials rarely make a cheaper building. Here's why."
- **Question:** "Do you know what your building should actually cost? Most owners don't."
- **How-to:** "How to budget a building before the design is even finished."
- **Story:** "A client almost lost $X to a missed quantity. Here's what we caught."

## Post template B — Carousel (LinkedIn / Instagram)

```
Slide 1 (cover): [Big promise] — e.g. "5 BOQ mistakes that cause disputes"
Slide 2: The problem (why it costs you)
Slide 3–6: One point per slide (tip + 1-line explanation)
Slide 7: Summary / checklist
Slide 8 (CTA): "Need an accurate BOQ? DM 'QUOTE' or visit [link]."
```

## Post template C — Reel / TikTok / Short (15–45s)

```
HOOK (0–3s, on screen + spoken): "This one mistake adds 10% to your build cost."
BODY (3–30s): explain ONE idea simply, show a drawing/site/number.
CTA (last 3s): "Follow for construction cost tips. Free quote — link in bio."
Caption: [hook] + 3–5 hashtags. Add captions/subtitles.
```

## YouTube video structure

```
0:00 Hook — the problem / promise
0:15 Who this helps + what they'll learn
0:45 Main content (3–5 clear points, show drawings/spreadsheets)
[mid] Quick example from a real (anonymised) project
[end] Recap + CTA: "Need this done? Link in description. Subscribe."
Title: keyword-first, e.g. "How to Estimate Construction Cost (Step by Step)"
Description: summary + timestamps + website link + services
```
