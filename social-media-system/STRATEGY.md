# Social Media Strategy

A personal-branding system to position **Eng. Muhiadin Ali** as the go-to expert in
quantity surveying, structural engineering and construction cost consulting — and
to generate consistent leads.

> build smarter with accurate BOQs, takeoffs & cost estimates | Free quote 👇

## Platform strategy

| Platform | Role | Format | Cadence |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **LinkedIn** | #1 — B2B authority & leads | Text + carousels, articles | 4–5×/week |
| **YouTube** | Deep authority, evergreen SEO | Tutorials, explainers (5–12 min) | 1×/week |

**Priority order:** LinkedIn → YouTube → Instagram/TikTok → Facebook.
Start strong on LinkedIn (where your buyers are); repurpose everywhere else.

## Content pillars (categories)

1. **Structural Engineering** — design logic, safety, materials.
2. **Quantity Surveying** — measurement, BOQs, the QS role.
3. **Construction Costs** — estimating, budgeting, saving money.
4. **BOQ Tips** — practical, tactical BOQ how-tos.
5. **Site Engineering** — real-world site lessons, drawings.
6. **Project Management** — planning, programmes, cost control.
7. **Personal Branding** — your story, behind-the-scenes, client wins.

## Content mix (the 5-3-2 rule per 10 posts)

- **5 educational** (tips, how-tos, mistakes to avoid) → builds authority
- **3 inspirational / proof** (case studies, client wins, before/after) → builds trust
- **2 promotional** (services, CTA, "DM me for a quote") → drives leads

## Content formats that perform

- **Carousels:** "5 BOQ mistakes", "How to estimate a building" (LinkedIn/IG).
- **Reels/Shorts:** one tip in 30s, site walk-throughs, "what this drawing tells me".
- **Before/After:** design or cost optimisation outcomes.
- **Talking-head:** explain a concept simply (great for YouTube + repurposing).
- **Story/poll:** "What's your biggest cost worry?" → engagement + research.

## Lead generation loop

1. **Educate** with free, specific tips (build trust + reach).
2. **Prove** with real projects & client outcomes.
3. **Invite** with a clear CTA ("DM 'QUOTE'", link in bio → contact page).
4. **Convert** via WhatsApp/contact form on the website.
5. **Repurpose** each piece across all platforms.

## Profile optimization checklist

- [ ] Consistent name & handle across platforms
- [ ] Professional headshot (same on all) + branded banner
- [ ] Keyword-rich bio + clear CTA + website link
- [ ] Link in bio → `contact.html` (or a link-in-bio page)
- [ ] Featured/pinned post = your best educational piece + offer
- [ ] Contact button / WhatsApp enabled where supported

## Metrics to track

- Reach & impressions (awareness)
- Engagement rate (resonance)
- Profile visits & follower growth (interest)
- Link clicks & DMs (intent)
- **Quote requests / leads (the only metric that pays)** ← track the source
